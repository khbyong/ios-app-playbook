# How To Make SwiftUI Apps Feel Native

Native feel is mostly about restraint and judgment.

## What Helps

- use expected navigation patterns
- respect safe areas and readable width
- keep primary actions obvious
- use semantic hierarchy for text
- support Dynamic Type and dark mode
- use sheets, lists, menus, and swipe actions where they fit

## What Breaks Native Feel

- web-style everything-is-a-card layouts
- custom navigation without a strong reason
- motion that delays task completion
- fake platform affordances
- cramped spacing and tiny tap targets

## A Fast Native-Feel Pass

1. check navigation
2. check spacing and hierarchy
3. check controls and hit areas
4. check dark mode and large text
5. check empty, loading, and error states

If any of those feel improvised, the app will feel less native than you think.
