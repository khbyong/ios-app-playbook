# iOS Motion And Microinteractions

Use motion to explain, confirm, or guide. Not to show off.

## Good Motion

- helps users track a state change
- makes hierarchy clearer
- confirms a successful interaction
- is short enough to not delay utility

## Good Haptics

- success on real completion
- warning on invalid action
- impact only when it adds confidence

## Avoid

- large decorative entrance animations
- long spring effects on utility screens
- haptics for every tap
- motion that competes with content
