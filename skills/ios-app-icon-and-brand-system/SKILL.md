# iOS App Icon And Brand System

Strong brand does not need to fight the platform.

## Icon Rules

- keep the silhouette simple
- design for tiny sizes first
- avoid text if possible
- prefer one strong symbol or shape
- test against home screen contexts, not just on white artboards

## Brand System Rules

- one primary accent color
- one calm background strategy
- one clear typographic hierarchy
- consistent icon treatment across product and marketing

## Avoid

- over-detailed icons
- raw bitmap edges where masking assumptions matter
- one brand style in-app and another in screenshots
- branding so strong it breaks native trust
