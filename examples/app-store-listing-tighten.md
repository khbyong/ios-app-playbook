# App Store Listing Tighten

## Problem

A listing tried to sound broad and powerful, but the promise became vague and less trustworthy.

## Weak Version

- subtitle: `Smart AI Finance Assistant`
- screenshot headline: `Everything you need to manage money`

## Stronger Version

- subtitle: `Daily Expense Tracker`
- screenshot headline: `Log what you spend and see it instantly`

## Why It Is Better

- it describes the real primary job of the app
- it is faster to understand in search and screenshot contexts
- it improves trust because the listing matches the first-run experience

## Playbook Skills Behind It

- `ios-app-store-optimization`
- `ios-app-icon-optimization`
