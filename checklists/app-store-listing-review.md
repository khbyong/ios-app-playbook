# App Store Listing Review

- The icon is recognizable at small size
- The icon is distinct from close competitors
- The first screenshot explains the core value immediately
- Screenshot headlines are concrete and easy to scan
- The title and subtitle describe the real use case
- Metadata does not promise unfinished or unclear features
- The listing language matches the in-app experience
- Promotional text is useful, not filler
- The product page feels trustworthy, not over-optimized
